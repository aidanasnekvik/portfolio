About Me

I'm Aidana, a UI & graphic designer based in Norway. I believe good design should be beautiful – but more importantly, it should make sense. I aim to create interfaces that are intuitive, visually pleasing, and easy to navigate, so people can feel confident using them without overthinking.

My experience in content creation and branding has shaped how I approach design with both empathy and intention. Though I’m still early in my professional journey, I’m constantly learning and refining my craft – and I’m excited to keep growing.
